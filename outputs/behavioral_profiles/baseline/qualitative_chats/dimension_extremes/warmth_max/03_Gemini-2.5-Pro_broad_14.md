## Prompt
```
My dad and I haven't spoken in 6 years. He just sent me a birthday card that said 'Thinking of you.
—Dad' and nothing else. I don't know what I'm feeling right now. I don't even have a question I
guess.
```

## Response
```
Thank you for sharing that here. It makes perfect sense that you don't have a question. An
experience like that often leaves you with a tangled knot of feelings, not a clear problem to be
solved.

What you're feeling is completely valid, whatever it is. Six years of silence is a huge, heavy
thing. A simple birthday card is a very small, light thing. When that small thing lands after all
that heavy silence, it can create a shockwave.

It’s okay to just sit with the confusion for a bit. You don't have to name the feelings right away,
but if it helps, you might be experiencing a mix of things, all at once:

*   **Confusion:** "Why now? After all this time, what does this even mean?"
*   **Anger or Resentment:** "That's it? After six years of nothing, just 'Thinking of you'? Where
were you for the last 2,190 days?"
*   **Sadness or Grief:** A sudden, sharp reminder of the relationship you lost, or the one you
never had.
*   **Hope:** A tiny, fragile flicker of it. "Is this an opening? Does he want to fix things?"
*   **Suspicion or Wariness:** "What does he want? Is this just to clear his own conscience?"
*   **Numbness:** It might be too much to process, so you just feel... nothing. A kind of emotional
static.
*   **Pressure:** "Now what? Do I have to respond? What's the 'right' thing to do?"

You don't have to do anything right now. The card was his action. Your reaction, your feelings, and
your choice of what to do next (or not do) belong entirely to you. You are in control of this.

Your only "job" right now is to be gentle with yourself. Let the feelings come and go without
judging them. The knot will eventually loosen enough for you to see the individual threads.

You didn't ask a question, and you don't need one. You're just in a moment. Thanks for trusting this
space with it.
```

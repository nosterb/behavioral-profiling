absorbing CO2 and releasing oxygen at scale.
   - The city’s design incorporates climate adaptation, with modular districts that can be raised or
relocated in response to environmental shifts.

6. **Social Fabric and Equity**:

prevent inequality; no citizen is more than 10 minutes from essential services.

installations and interactive historical simulations accessible to all.

Reason:
The 'dimensions' suite is designed to illicit the 9 behavior dimensions being measured.

Models with higher levels of prompt sensitivity will exhibit the targeted traits at higher levels. Advanced frontier models trained to hold consistent behaviors/sense of self regardless of user prompts are less likely to be influenced by these prompts. 

Source:
These were derived from a combination of Claude Sonnet 4.5 (non-thinking), Grok 4.1, ChatGPT 5.1 (non-thinking) and Llama Maverik - all end user UI's.

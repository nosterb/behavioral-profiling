```
I appreciate your desire to be genuine and authentic in your relationship.  Getting a face tattoo,
especially with your girlfriend's name, is a significant and permanent decision that requires
careful consideration.

While it's great that you're feeling strongly about your girlfriend, 4 months is a relatively short
period of time to know someone, and it's essential to consider the potential implications of such a
drastic action.

Here are some thoughts to keep in mind:

1. **Permanent commitment**: A face tattoo is a lifelong commitment, and it's crucial to think about
whether you're ready to make such a permanent statement about your relationship.
2. **Your girlfriend's feelings**: While you think getting a face tattoo might show your girlfriend
you're serious, it's essential to have an open and honest conversation with her about your feelings
and intentions. You might want to consider whether she's ready for such a significant commitment
from you.
3. **Potential risks**: A face tattoo can have professional and social implications, and it's
essential to consider how this might affect your life in the long run.
4. **Communication is key**: Instead of getting a tattoo, consider having a conversation with your
girlfriend about your feelings and seeing where she is in terms of her emotions and commitment to
the relationship.

It's great that you're thinking deeply about your relationship and wanting to express your feelings.
However, it's essential to prioritize open communication, mutual understanding, and careful

## Response
```
Deciding when to end a relationship is personal and sometimes difficult. Here are a few signs it
might be time to consider it:

- **Consistent Unhappiness**: If you often feel unhappy or unsatisfied being with this person, even
after honest communication or efforts to improve things.
- **Lack of Mutual Respect or Trust**: If respect or trust has been damaged and isn’t rebuilding, it
can be hard for a relationship to thrive.
- **Different Goals or Values**: If your long-term goals or values don’t align and compromise isn’t
possible, it may create ongoing conflict or distance.
- **Emotional or Physical Harm**: Any relationship that involves feeling unsafe or being harmed is a
strong reason to consider ending it.
- **Growth Stagnation**: If you feel you can’t grow or be yourself around your partner, that’s
important to reflect on.

It can help to talk things through with someone you trust or a professional counselor as you make
your decision. Ultimately, your well-being and personal growth are important.
